Intro

My name is Jonathan Butler and I have spent my entire professional life until now as a classical cellists, performing concerts all over the world. I completed my doctorate of musical arts in April, writing my dissertation on new teaching methods for young cellists, harnessing their natural creative impulses.
I live in Los Angeles and perform regularly as a studio musician in the local film/TV studios.
Working as a cello teacher was something I loved because every student brought me a unique set of problems each week, and it was my responsibility to be empathetic, ask the right questions, identify the cause of the student's problems and make sure they walked away with a personalized set of strategies to conquer their challenges that week.

About me

Since I was a teenager, I have been enthusiastic about a functional, beautiful website design. I love to define concepts of beauty and functionality and translate them onto the internet.
As a professional musician, I have a deep undertanding of the importance of team chemistry. My aim is to be a contributor to the team's highest goals.
My other interests include specialty coffee (particularly geisha crops and natural processed beans) and fitness (particularly slow, controlled body-weight exercises).
I am a full stack front end web developer. Skills include HTML, CSS, Javascript, React, and Node.
